Himalaya International School (HIS) Bangana is a Co-education Day boarding school. It is a day school for pre-primary students and day boarding facilities start from class 1.
HIS Bangana located in Shivalik hills of Himalayan range, a peaceful, safe and surrounded through green hills. The school will provide students with academic opportunities along with technological innovations.
HIS Bangana have spaces for the development of talents, entertainment, sports and culture. Apart from indoor activities for preprimary students, the school will shortly provide outdoor activities like football ground, cricket ground, basketball ground, volleyball ground, tennis court, badminton court, and swimming pool in coming days.
Our dedicated team of experienced teaches is committed to providing a well-rounded education that encourages curiosity, exploration, and discovery.
We believe in the importance of a well-rounded curriculum that includes not only core subjects like math, science, and language arts, but also art, music, physical education, and other enrichment activities.
Our aim is to help our students develop the knowledge, skills, and confidence they need to succeed in the 21st century.
Imagine, it is 2030. What skill-sets will our current Year 3 learners need to either further their education or enter the workforce?
That’s quite a challenge, isn’t it? As far as education goes, we are utilizing STEAM as an approach to future-proof our learners for this day.
STEAM education, simply put, is the integration of science, technology, engineering, arts and mathematics. We focus on STEAM subjects in schools so that our young adults are equipped with the necessary skills for the economy of the future.
Increasingly, employers are looking for workers who are creative problem solvers, innovative and critical thinkers, and able to use new technologies.
Such challenges mean STEAM is viewed as a way to provide an exciting opportunity for inquiry learning through the integration of curriculum and a design thinking approach that is engaging and relevant to students.
Himalaya International School Bangana, each one on campus will be trained to make safety not only a habit, but a culture.
The school is well equipped with staff to handle unexpected events or incidents. All members of staff and students will be trained on aspects of Fire Safety, Disaster Management and Crisis Control.
